9) A blonde walks into a pharmacy and asks the assistant for some rectum deodorant. The pharmacist, a little bemused, explains to the woman they don't sell rectum deodorant, and never have. Unfazed, the blonde assures the pharmacist that she has been buying the stuff from this store on a regular basis and would like some more.

Pharmacist: "I'm sorry. We don't have any!"
Blonde: "But I always buy it here."
Pharmacist: "Do you have the container that it came in?"
Blonde: "Yes, I'll go home and get it." She returns with the container and hands it to the pharmacist who looks at it and says to her,
Pharmacist: "This is just a normal stick of underarm deodorant."

Annoyed, the blonde snatches the container back and reads out aloud from the container "TO APPLY, PUSH UP BOTTOM."

10) Bubba dies in a fire and his body is pretty badly burned. The morgue sends for his two best friends, Daryl and Gomer, to identify the body. Daryl arrives first, and when the mortician pulls back the sheet.

Daryl: "Yup, his face is burnt up pretty bad. You better roll him over."

The mortician rolls him over.

Daryl: "Nope, ain't Bubba."

The mortician thinks this is strange. Then he brings Gomer in to identify the body. Gomer takes a look at the face and says, "Yup, he's pretty well burnt up. Roll him over."
The mortician rolls him over.

Gomer: "No, it ain't Bubba."
The mortician: "How can you tell?"
Gomer: "Well, Bubba had two a**holes."
The mortician: "What? He had two a**holes?!"
Gomer: "Yup, every time we went to town, folks would say, 'Here comes Bubba with them two a**holes.''
